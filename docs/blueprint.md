# **App Name**: Toda Bela Storefront

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Display: Showcase products with images, names, current prices, previous prices, and promotional badges, categorized for easy browsing.
- Product Category Browsing: Allow users to filter and browse products by different categories like 'Vestidos' and 'Novidades'.
- Shopping Cart Functionality: Enable users to add products to a shopping cart and view cart contents.
- Newsletter Subscription: Provide a form for users to subscribe to a newsletter, capturing emails for marketing and updates.
- Dynamic Content Management: Integrate with a Firebase backend (Firestore) to dynamically manage product listings, categories, banners, and promotional content.
- AI Product Description Tool: Provide an AI tool for administrators to generate engaging and relevant product descriptions based on basic product details, streamlining content creation.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary brand color, a sophisticated deep plum, draws from a refined and warm feminine aesthetic, evoking confidence and elegance. HEX: #6E3C47.
- A delicate, pale blush serves as the predominant background color, maintaining a clean and airy feel. HEX: #FDF6F8.
- A muted rose-magenta is used for accents and interactive elements, providing a vibrant yet harmonious contrast that enhances key actions. HEX: #E09FD6.
- Headlines utilize 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, for a modern, clean, and assertive brand voice.
- Body text is set in 'Inter', a highly readable grotesque sans-serif, ensuring clarity and an objective, accessible feel across all content.
- Implement minimalist, elegant line icons that reflect the modern and sophisticated visual identity without overwhelming the design.
- A clean, responsive grid-based layout prioritizes visual hierarchy and product presentation, offering an intuitive browsing experience across devices with ample whitespace.
- Subtle, smooth animations and hover effects on interactive elements provide engaging visual feedback, enhancing the user experience with gentle sophistication.
